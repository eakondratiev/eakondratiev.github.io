Mouse Events

Sometimes the mouse can produce several clicks instead of one. It is known as misclicks. The cause could be anything from the mechanical wear of mouse switches to a poor RF or Bluetooth connection. This page monitors left and right button clicks and detects whether they are too frequent. Also, it tracks other events of a pointing device like a touchscreen or a trackpad.
